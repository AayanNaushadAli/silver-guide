# CS401: Cyber Security - Unit 1 Notes
## Topic: Introduction to Information Security

### Key Concepts:
1. **Confidentiality**: Ensuring that data is accessible only to those authorized to have access.
2. **Integrity**: Maintaining and assuring the accuracy and completeness of data over its entire life-cycle.
3. **Availability**: Ensuring that information and communication services are available when needed.

### The CIA Triad
The CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) is a model designed to guide policies for information security within an organization.

### Cyber Attacks vs Cyber Threats
- **Threat**: A potential danger that may exploit a vulnerability.
- **Attack**: An active attempt to deliberate exploit a system's vulnerability.
